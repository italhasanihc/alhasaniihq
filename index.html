Some companies fill spaces.

Al-Hasna Group is Iraq's leading name in luxury home living — a brand built on quality craftsmanship, refined design, and a deep understanding of what makes a space feel truly special.

Based in Baghdad, we have spent years helping people reimagine the way they live, working to a standard that is consistent, considered, and recognized across the industry. We are more than a furniture group — we bring together a carefully selected portfolio of brands, a strong design sensibility, and an honest commitment to quality visible in every detail of every space we work on.

Our clients include architects, developers, and discerning individuals who come to us with a vision. We bring it to life with precision, good taste, and a clear sense of what luxury actually means in practice — to create spaces that are not just beautiful, but genuinely memorable.

Our Vision

Our Vision

Design is more than furniture. It is a way of life.

Est. 1998 Baghdad

Luxury is not defined by price, but by permanence, intention, and the quiet power of extraordinary spaces.

At Al-Hasna Group, we believe design is more than furniture — it is a way of life. Rooted in Baghdad and inspired by a civilization that shaped the meaning of beauty, we create environments where craftsmanship, culture, and vision come together with precision and purpose.

Our work is guided by discipline, refined taste, and a belief that the home is the most important space a person inhabits. Every detail, every line, and every material is chosen to elevate the everyday and transform living spaces into lasting experiences.

We do not follow trends. We create spaces that hold their meaning over time — interiors built for those who recognize the difference between what is simply beautiful and what is truly exceptional.

Al-Hasna Group exists to redefine modern Iraqi living, crafting spaces designed to be lived in with pride and remembered for generations.
