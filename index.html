Who We Are

About
Al-Hasna Group

0 Years Shaping Iraq's Finest Brands

Founded on principles of quality and vision, Al-Hasna Group has grown into one of Iraq's most respected corporate conglomerates. From our roots in Baghdad, we have built an ecosystem of brands that serve hundreds of thousands of customers each year.

Our approach is rooted in a deep understanding of what modern consumers deserve — premium products, transparent service, and spaces that inspire. Every decision we make is anchored in our unwavering commitment to elevating the standard of living.

We operate across retail, interior design, outdoor living, and fashion verticals, guided by a unified culture of excellence and a passion for creating meaningful experiences.
